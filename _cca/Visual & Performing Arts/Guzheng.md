<p><strong>Tradition is beautiful!</strong>
</p>
<p>Oasis Guzheng Ensemble strives to inculcate a deep artistic appreciation
for the 2000-year-old traditional Chinese instrument. This CCA brings out
the best in teamwork, discipline and commitment, and it encourages every
member to work together, forge friendships and learn skills that will last
a lifetime. As we make beautiful music as a team, we hope to move hearts
and inspire many others to desire playing the Guzheng.</p>
